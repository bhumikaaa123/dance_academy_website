ABOUT US

JOIN US TODAY FOR YOUR FIRST DANCE CLASS

Lets Naccho Academy was established in 2010 with the vision of creating a nurturing and dynamic environment where dance enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels can thrive. Founded by Bhumika Tusamad, a passionate dancer with years of experience, our academy is dedicated to providing high-quality dance education and fostering a community of creativity, expression, and discipline.

Since our inception, Lets Naccho Academy has been a beacon of excellence in dance education, offering a diverse range of dance styles including Ballet, Contemporary, Jazz, Hip Hop, and Classical Dance. Our highly skilled instructors are not only experts in their respective fields but also mentors who inspire and guide our students to reach their full potential.
